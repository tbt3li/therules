    Julia / Gravel

    FIRST HERO: First, choose your hero.
    You will be able to change them later on. Gravel and Julia don’t give you any special abilities yet.

    Starter Guardians
    Alrick

    Evil gaze and a popular hero in the Total Battle universe. With Alrick, a march to your city takes at least 4 hours.
    Please note: – A march to a city in the kingdom (from edge to edge) takes approximately 2 hours. – With boosters, the duration can be reduced to just 7.5 minutes.
    Recommendation: If you are often online, then this hero might be just right for you. But if you are asleep, you will certainly not wake up in 7.5 minutes 😉

    Time Lock Master
    Thaddeus

    A Caesar-like hero for your attacks.
    Thaddeus gives you +25% leadership. This means you can take more guards/specialists with you on an attack or march.
    This hero is very useful at the beginning. However, over time, he becomes an expensive companion in events such as Armageddon.
    Please note: As an attacker, you cannot take as many troops with you as you can produce as a defender until you fall into a red food zone.
    Thaddeus is therefore not as popular with larger players.

    Army Commander
    Mathemhain

    An unused engineer booster
    With this hero, your engineers receive a +200% boost to strength and health. The delay in restoring walls is also reduced by 30 seconds.
    When are engineers needed? Fortress attacks and player attacks. The wall takes 30 minutes to rebuild. Does 30 seconds really matter? 😉

    Engineer Booster
    Haemon

    Haemon is a hero you don’t see very often.
    He doubles all active clan bonuses. Why is he often forgotten? Most clans simply activate bonuses across the board. This can be useful at first, but later on, players usually prefer strength or superiority.
    But note: the clan can levy a bonus for 1 day, during which +25 army strength is effective. If you double this strength, Haemon is indeed a weapon.

    Bonus Amplifier
    Meriones

    Not very popular, but very practical
    This hero gives you bonuses for your equipment. Once you can equip this hero with very good equipment, he can give you strength. The equipment is improved by 1.5 times. So you get 50% more out of your equipment 😉
    But note: as soon as this hero’s equipment is of poor quality, you will most likely want to change him.

    Gear Enhancer
    Faron

    A hated hero in the Total Battle universe. But why?
    In many kingdoms, this hero is even banned by the ROE. That’s because he gives you an extreme boost in the battle for the throne. If you are in the Senate, your vote counts 10 times! This means you have an enormous influence on the election! That’s why this hero is so unpopular!

    Throne Influencer
    Thelensia

    Want to enjoy gifts like it’s Christmas? Here’s Thelensia
    With this Hero, you’ll receive a box of random gifts every 20 hours. With a little luck, these could be Mercenaries & Gold gifts. Or they could just be resources.
    Please note: The gifts become more expensive and better the higher your hero level is. The division is in 50-level increments.
    Recommendation: So you want to try your luck? And would you like to be gifted like a king? Then this might be a good choice for you.

    Gifts Queen
    Ayrin

    Are you in a kingdom with ROE that does not allow attacks? Then Ayrin is your opportunity.
    She enables you to march into all kingdoms.
    She does not come with any additional bonuses. It accompanies you on every march. It doesn’t matter how long it lasts.
    Recommendation: However, if you simply want to go exploring from time to time, she is the best choice.

    Kingdoms Explorer
    Svyatogor

    Want strength and health? But beware!
    With this hero, your entire army will receive a strength and health boost. But why beware? This bonus does not apply to every attack, because the fine print should not be overlooked here.
    Therefore, please note: Under Svyatogor’s command, the bonus is ONLY active when he is marching without captains. What does that mean? In the case of a rare monster squad, Armageddon, arena runs

    Solo Powerhouse
    Ryjong

    As fast as Speedy Gonzales?
    This hero lets you dart across the world map and quickly carry out your explorations and attacks. He doubles your marching speed. This can be useful in Battle of Kingdoms.
    But when facing a strong opponent, wouldn’t you rather have security through strength?

    Speed Master
    GuanYu

    Unpopular due to low strength
    This hero can give you an overall advantage. As soon as you attack a player who has the same level of guards, specialists, or monsters, your troops can strike first. This means that your troops may kill his strongest units before they get to you.
    But note: as soon as the attacker has guards or specialists that are one level better, the first strike is not yours.

    First Strike
    Yamato

    A hero for double attacks
    This hero may be somewhat underestimated or even somewhat disliked. If you go on a spy attack and are successful, you will kill the enemy’s captain. They will not be able to revive them for 20 minutes.
    But is it only the hero who makes the difference? Because there are also captains with far better skills.

    Silent Assassin
    Nectarius

    Often chosen—then changed after all.
    This hero sounds really great at first glance. He boosts your holy potions The production of potions is increased and you can produce a few more potions in your city yourself. Is that enough? It may be a good bonus at first. But even here, the potions will not be enough for you.
    But note: Once the temple has reached 1500, production will stop here as well. This bonus is therefore inactive.
