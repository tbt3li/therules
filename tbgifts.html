This page lists the daily and one-time community gifts for Total Battle.
The Daily Gifts work once a week, on the given day. The One-Time Gifts work only once. :)
Make sure you choose the correct link for each gift!
  • If you play on PC or in a Web Browser, choose "Browser/Total Battle"
  • If you play on IPhone or Android, choose "Mobile"
  • If you play "Triumph - Dawn of Power", choose "Browser/Triumph"
On Android, the links will only work if clicking on the link starts the game. So make sure you close the game before clicking on a link!
On iOS (Apple Iphone), it seems that the links work, but it will take up to two days until you get your reward!
A "Total Battle Day" starts when the events (Doomsday,...) start. Note that some of the mobile links may not work.
Check the bottom of this page for contact information. If you know any links I missed, please send them to me! :)
