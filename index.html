About

Who we are

Welcome to [THE] Family Gang, a powerful and united clan in Kingdom 138 of Total Battle. We are a community of dedicated players who thrive on strategy, teamwork and mutual support. Our mission is to dominate the battlefield while fostering an environment where every member feels valued and empowered. Whether you are a seasoned warrior or a rising strategist, [THE] is your home for growth, camaraderie, and victory. Together, we conquer kingdoms and etch our legacy into the history of Total Battle. Join us, and become part of something extraordinary!

Our Characteristics:

  • Team Spirit: We work together to achieve collective success.
  • Strategic Excellence: We plan and execute tactics to outsmart our enemies.
  • Inclusivity: We welcome and support players of all skill levels.
  • Resilience: We overcome challenges with determination and strength.
  • Unity: We stay loyal and united in every battle and beyond.
