1. DEFINITIONS:
Rules of Engagement (ROE): The kingdom law which governs fair play within K138
Inter Kingdom Fair play Agreement (IKFPA): Agreement among participating kingdoms for fair play.
Clan Territory: The border which surrounds a clan.
Clan Buildings: Clan forts, capital, Resource Production buildings.
RSS Tiles: Can be game spawned or player created; players send a captain and troops to collect the respective resources in the tile.
Clan RSS: RSS tile within a Clan Territory.
Wild RSS: RSS tile outside of Clan Territories.
Wild: Area outside of clan territories.
Dragon Mounds (DM): Game spawned RSS tile which contains Gold to be collected by a player.
Gold Mines: Player Generated to mine for gold. They are always protected and require permission from the owner to be mined.
Might: A nominal metric used to evaluate a player's strength.
Diplomacy: Conversation to resolve conflict.
Compensation: Payment to resolve loss or inconvenience.
Peace Time: The time between each Clash for the Throne (COT).
Clash for the Throne (COT): 48 hour tournament when players gain points via attacks on other players.
Conquest Points (CP): Points obtained through attacks on other players.
CP Exchange: An agreed attack for the sole purpose of gaining Conquest Points. This can be arranged player vs. player or via clan march on one player with significant Might.
Spam Attacks: More than 3 consecutive attacks from a single player within a 6 hour timeframe.
Clan March: A joint attack on a target using a single march.

ALL-TIME LAW

All players are considered to be under the umbrella of Kingdom 138 and are subject to the rules of the kingdom. As unique situations arise, the Royal Council can rule on a fair and just solution to accommodate all parties and the kingdom.

2. CODE OF CONDUCT
A) Remember this is a game, meant for enjoyment and relief from everyday life and stress.
B) Every player is a human being deserving respect and common decency.
C) No racism, bullying, targeting of specific players for hate speech or written abuse will be tolerated. Accounts will be reported to the Total Battle Administration.
3. PORTALS
Memorial Portals are never to be attacked, even if an attack comes from them. Clan Portals and Active Event Portals are protected during Peace Time.
4. CONDOLENCE MINES (Update)
CM.1) This law applies to any type of mine built instead of memorial portals.
CM.2) Mines of this type must be located on the clan border or near the mourner's city.
CM.3) Players with less than 20M of power may harvest these mines.
5. CLAN TERRITORY
CT.1) Clan RSS tiles may be defended by the clan at all times.
CT.2) If a clan's borders overtake another clan's fort, the affected clan must relocate their fort within 48 hours.
CT.2.a) The clan who absorbs the fort must notify the clan who owns the fort to request they relocate the fort. At this point, the 48 hour timer starts.
CT.2.b) If no agreement is made after 24 hours, the clans can notify the Royal Council to advise on next steps for the remaining 24 hours.
CT.3) Scouting and attacks on clan buildings are an act of war and forbidden.
6. RESOURCES (RSS)
R.1) All Wild RSS are first march, first served. Speed up your marches to resource tile. DO NOT race to the location if someone is already marching there, forcing them to attack you.
R.2) Clan RSS tiles are property of the clan and can be defended at all times. This includes: all RSS (silver, wood, stone, iron, gold, tar) and also all player built resources.
R.3) No attacking occupied RSS tiles, unless defending clan territory.
R.4) Arena's, crypts, citadels, and monsters are not defined as RSS tiles and are not protected.
R.5) No occupying Gold Mines without the consent of the Gold Mine owner.
7. GOLD INGOTS
Gold ingots have no individual use but are vital for kingdom-wide bonuses. To maintain a sufficient supply:
G.1) Clan leaders must remind players to deposit ingots in the Treasury and spot-check compliance.
G.2) Accruing ingots in cities makes players and clans targets for other kingdoms.
G.3) Royal Court can arrange an attack on cities holding over 50K ingots with open portals or without a peace shield in an effort to keep the ingots in 138.
G.4) Players will be messaged with a 3 minute warning and a scout to confirm they are online and alert for any incoming attacks by other kingdoms.
8. DRAGON MOUNDS
DM.1) Dragon mounds in clan territory belong to that clan.
DM.2) Wild Dragon Mounds must be registered in K138DM. chat using the y coordinates, prior to possession.
DM.3) A player must march immediately AFTER registering, otherwise it becomes invalid. Time between registering & arrival to the Wild DM must be within 10 Mins.
DM.4) Any unregistered players occupying Wild DMs can be removed by the newly registered owner, DM Chat Supervisors or Royal Guard.
DM.5) Once a player's troops leave the Wild DM, the registration process begins again.
DM.5.a) exception is in the event of an attack on a registered player/Wild DM. Once the attacker is removed, they have 10 min to reoccupy the Wild DM. No need to re-register.
DM.6)Maximum of 1 Wild DM is allowed to be occupied by a player per game reset.
9. ROYAL GUARDS
Takes action at the request of the royal council to fulfill disciplinary action on 138 severe violations (specific to 138 kingdom conflicts); pursues outlaws inside K138.
10. INTER KINGDOM EXPECTATIONS
K138 is not part of any Inter Kingdom Agreement.
Do not attack Memorial Portals and Clan Buildings. If K138 Clan Buildings are attacked, K138 will retaliate swiftly and relentlessly.
11. OUTLAWS
O.1) Players who violate the ROE, attack clan buildings, or do not pay their judgments may be declared Outlaw by the King, or by agreement of the Royal Council. Royal Guard may be asked to take action prior to issuing outlaw status. Outlaw players lose ROE protection and must be listed in Outlaws chat.
O.2) Attacks on clan buildings or violations of the code conduct can result in immediate outlaw status.
O.3) Clans harboring outlaws have 24 hours to remove outlaw players or pay outlaw debts to avoid Outlaw Clan status. Outlawed clans are not protected by ROE, but clan buildings are still protected unless announced in the outlaw decree.
12. PEACE TIME
PT.1) All-Time law applies.
PT.2) All Intrakingdom scouting and attacks are forbidden, with the exception of protecting Clan RSS tiles.
PT.3) In the event of an Intrakingdom attack, an equal retaliation attack is permitted or diplomacy can be utilized to agree upon compensation.
PT.4) Diplomacy - player to player resolution. If there is no solution after 24 hours, escalate to clan diplomats/leaders. After 24 additional hours, it can be escalated to the Royal Council to advise on next steps. Royal Council will not get involved until all efforts have been made over 48 hours by posting the attack report and a 1 post description of the efforts to resolve in the chat: 138.Attacks.
13. COT
C.1) All-time law applies.
C.2) Attacks permitted between players 100K - 1M might, 1M - 5M might; greater than 5M might.
C.3) Spam Attacks are forbidden.
C.4) Portal attacks permitted if the portal is in the wild, not an Active Event Portal nor Memorial Portal.
C.5) Hostile Portals can be attacked regardless of the might of the portal owner. Clan Portals and active Event Portals can be closed if more than 2 attacks originate from them regardless of the might of the portal owner.
C.6) Clan marches are permitted on players with over 25M might.
C.7) CP exchanges are encouraged between players and clans.
C.8) If exploring other kingdoms, players are expected to follow the inter-kingdom expectations.
Note ** If you do not wish to participate in COT, then use a peace shield or send your troops to a clan fort and keep your resources below the protected thresholds.
14. SUMMARY
S.1) Clan RSS tiles can be defended by clans.
S.2) Peace time means no attacks outside of defending clan territory.
S.3) Wild Dragon Mounds must be registered prior to possession.
S.4) COT - attacks only within your bracket or above. No clan marches on players below 25M might.
S.5) Use diplomacy to settle disputes when possible.
