      1. Respect
      1.1 Be respectful to all players‚Äîno bullying, harassment, or discrimination of any kind will be tolerated.

      2.1 Maintain respectful and clear communication at all times.
      2.2 Keep all event and clan chats free of spam.
      2.3 Moderators will issue warnings for off-topic or obstructive messages. Continued violations may lead to removal from the clan.

      3. Kingdom Rules
      3.1 Obey all kingdom rules.
      3.2 Breaking kingdom rules may result in immediate expulsion.

      4. Clan Reputation
      4.1 Any interactions with enemies that could affect the clan‚Äôs image must be discussed with leadership beforehand. Unauthorized actions will result in removal.

      5. Stay Informed & Absences
      5.1 Regularly check the journal, clan chats, and Message of the Day (MotD).
      5.2 Players ignoring messages for 3+ days while online will be warned; 5+ days without response or notice may result in removal.
      5.3 Notify leadership of any absences as early as possible, preferably in the ‚ÄúAbsent-THE‚Äù chat, with estimated duration.

      6. Resource Ownership and Registration
      6.1 Players must register Silver Villages or Gold Mines they want to keep exclusive.
      6.2 Unregistered villages/mines are considered free for occupation.
      6.3 If occupying someone‚Äôs registered property, vacate promptly and provide fair compensation.
      6.4 Do not register game-generated Silver Villages.
      6.5 Always check Silver/Gold Registration chats before occupying any site.
      6.6 Dragon Mounds and Wellsprings must be registered according to Kingdom Rules of Engagement (K123-RoE).

      7. Active Contribution
      7.1 No freeloaders. Players only farming without contributing will be removed.
      7.2 Contributions include sending resources/silver to clan buildings and securing chests from crypts, arenas, citadels, etc. (Level 5 and 10 regular crypts do not count).

      8. Leadership Exclusivity
      8.1 Leadership members cannot hold leadership or superior roles in other clans to avoid conflicts of interest.
      8.2 Violations may result in removal from the leadership team.

      9. Resource Protection
      9.1 Keep silver/resources below protected limits to avoid frequent attacks.
      9.2 Contact leadership for guidance if unsure about protection limits.

      10. Kingdom Rules
      10.1 Do not share clan-related messages or attack reports externally without leadership approval (personal reports exempt).

      11. War Time
      11.1 During war, all players must protect the clan and act quickly as needed.
      11.2 Players must participate actively in war events.
      11.3 Alternate or seasonal accounts found online but not assisting the main clan during war, or players avoiding participation, will be removed and banned from rejoining.
      11.4 Players unwilling to stand with the clan during war should leave voluntarily. There is no place for fair-weather members.
      11.5 Exemptions due to unavoidable real-life circumstances must be requested through leadership.
      11.6 All main clan members must have kingdom teleporters ready within 35 days of joining or rejoining.

    • Players Ranking in [THE] Clan

      Superior:
      Only players with active duties can hold this rank.

      Examples of duties include: Diplomats, Buildings/Research, Recruiters, CP Run organizers, etc.

      Exception: During the "Clash of Kingdoms" event, players who plan to hold banners may request to become Superiors for 24 hours, allowing them to kick their city out of the clan territory if needed. During this time, they are not allowed to interfere with any other clan duties, accept requests, kick players, or perform any other actions. If they break this rule, they will be immediately removed from the clan.

      Note: *The rank is not based on the time spent in the clan.

      Officer:
      Players in this rank must have a strong understanding of the kingdom and clan rules, as well as the inner regulations regarding clan events (such as Ancient's Treasure, CP Run, etc.).

      They should be able to replace Superiors when needed, in case the Superior is unavailable or decides to quit their duties. Officers must be able to register vault attack reports, send the correct troops and captains to raids and CP Runs, and control the raids they initiate, ensuring all players send the proper troops and captains. They must also ensure that points are allocated correctly.

      Veteran:
      Players who know the clan and kingdom rules, as well as how to build and register the vault, but do not meet the other requirements for the Officer rank.

      Soldier:
      This is the rank for new players who are still adjusting to the clan, as well as players who have broken clan rules and were temporarily demoted to this rank.
